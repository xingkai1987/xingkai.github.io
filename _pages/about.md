Biography
======
Prof. Kai Xing is Head of Finance Department at the School of Economics and Management, Nanchang University. He received his PhD from Nottingham University Business School and worked as a postdoctoral researcher in the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science of the Chinese Academy of Sciences. 
He has been granted the project supported by Jiangxi Provincial Ganpo Talant Special Foundation, which is the highest-level talent program of Jiangxi Province. 
He also holds multiple esteemed roles including Council Member of the Chinese Finance Annual Meeting, Council Member of the Financial System Engineering Committee of the Systems Engineering Society of China, Member of the Asia-Pacific Applied Economics Association (APAEA), and Council Member of the FinTech Committee of the Chinese Society of Technical Economics. He is the founder of the International Academic Conference on Digital Finance (IACDF), Nanchang, China(https://sem.ncu.edu.cn/aifc_en/aifc_new_en/index.htm](https://sem.ncu.edu.cn/IACDF_2026.htm).
He serves the associate editor of International Review of Economics and Finance, subject editor of Emerging Markets Finance & Trade (EMFT) and guest editor of Journal of Accounting Literature, Pacific-Basin Finance Journal, EMFT and Journal of Chinese Economic and Business Studies. 
His research interests cover corporate finance, corporate governance as well as risk management. He has published more than 23 papers in International Review of Financial Analysis, Finance Research Letters, Economic Modelling etc. He has received research grants more than 550,000 US dollars from National Natural Science Foundation of China, National “242” Information Safety Project etc.


中文简历
======
邢凯，金融学博士（英国诺丁汉大学），博士后（中国科学院数学与系统科学研究院），校聘教授，硕导，金融学系主任，江西省“赣鄱英才计划”创新高端人才项目人选、中国金融学年会理事、中国系统工程学会金融系统工程专业委员会理事、亚太应用经济学会会员（APAEA）、中国技术经济学会金融科技专业委员会理事、南昌大学智慧金融决策与产业应用实验室主任、长江经济带研究院（中国人民大学）高级研究员、江西产业金融发展研究院执行院长、江西省普惠金融专家咨询委员会专家、江西省金融研究智库专家等，中国（南昌）亚洲国际金融年会（AIFC）发起人及秘书长，担任International Review of Economics and Finance副主编、Emerging Market Finance and Trade领域主编、Journal of Chinese Economic and Business Studies期刊编委；Journal of Accounting Literature、Pacific-Basin Finance Journal等4个期刊客座主编及20多个国内外期刊匿名审稿人。曾就职于中央网络安全和信息化委员会办公室国家计算机网络与信息安全管理中心。
主要研究方向风险管理、金融科技、公司金融等。主持国家自科青年1项、2项国家242信息安全专项等10项省部级以上项目。在International Review of Financial Analysis、Finance Research Letters、《系统工程理论与实践》、《计量经济学报》等期刊上发表学术论文二十余篇，包括ESI高被引论文一篇（1%）。在国内外知名学术会议荣获9项优秀论文奖，包括，第十五、二十、二十二届金融系统工程与风险管理国际年会、第七届中国优选法统筹法与经济数学研究会量化金融与保险分会学术年会等。
以第一主编身份出版教材三本《科技金融概论》、《金融衍生品理论与实务》、《电信网络诈骗原理与防范》，且均获批在高等教育出版社出版；参编翻译1本《国际金融（原书第16版）》并在机械工业出版社出版；3篇案例入选国家级案例库，其中，1篇入选全国百篇优秀管理案例。
负责研发证监会全国地方交易场所风险防控平台、中央网信办数字经济投融资联盟投融资信息服务平台、国家反诈大数据平台等，申请金融科技类国家发明专利7项。多次获党中央及江西省领导批示及部委感谢
