Biography
======
Prof. Kai Xing holds a PhD in Finance from the University of Nottingham and completed postdoctoral research at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He is the Head of the Department of Finance at Nanchang University and Director of the Nanchang University Laboratory of Intelligent Financial Decision-Making and Industrial Applications. He has been selected for the Jiangxi Provincial "Ganpo Talent Program" Innovative High-End Talent Project. His professional roles include Council Member of the Chinese Finance Annual Meeting, Council Member of the Financial Systems Engineering Committee of the Systems Engineering Society of China, Member of the Asia-Pacific Applied Economics Association (APAEA), Council Member of the FinTech Committee of the Chinese Society of Technical Economics, Senior Research Fellow at the Yangtze River Economic Belt Research Institute, Executive Dean of the Jiangxi Institute of Industrial Finance Development, Expert of the Jiangxi Provincial Inclusive Finance Expert Advisory Committee, and Expert of the Jiangxi Provincial Financial Research Think Tank. He is also the founder and secretary-general of the China (Nanchang) Asia International Finance Annual Conference (AIFC)<https://sem.ncu.edu.cn/IACDF_2026.htm>.   

He serves as Associate Editor of International Review of Economics and Finance, Subject Editor of Emerging Markets Finance and Trade, Editorial Board Member of Journal of Chinese Economic and Business Studies, Guest Editor of Journal of Accounting Literature and Pacific-Basin Finance Journal, and anonymous reviewer for more than 20 domestic and international journals. He previously worked at the National Computer Network and Information Security Management Center under the Office of the Central Cyberspace Affairs Commission.  

His research focuses on risk management, fintech, corporate finance, and corporate governance. He has led one National Natural Science Foundation of China Young Scientists Fund project and two National 242 Information Security Special Projects, along with multiple provincial and ministerial projects. He has published more than 23 academic papers in journals such as International Review of Financial Analysis, Finance Research Letters, Systems Engineering - Theory & Practice, and China Journal of Econometrics, including one ESI top 1% highly cited paper. He has received nine excellent-paper awards at major academic conferences in China and abroad.   

He has served as chief editor of three textbooks approved for publication by Higher Education Press: Introduction to Technology Finance, Theory and Practice of Financial Derivatives, and Principles and Prevention of Telecom and Online Fraud; and contributed to the Chinese edition of International Finance (16th original edition), published by China Machine Press. Three of his cases have been included in national case libraries, including one National Top 100 Excellent Management Case.   

He has led or played a principal role in developing the CSRC National Risk Monitoring and Prevention Platform for Local Trading Venues, the Digital Economy Investment and Financing Alliance Information Service Platform under the Office of the Central Cyberspace Affairs Commission, and the National Anti-Fraud Big-Data Platform. He has filed seven national fintech-related invention patents and has received written instructions from central and Jiangxi provincial leaders as well as letters of appreciation from ministries, commissions, and industry associations.
